## 3.2.3 Activity

```sql
SELECT PS_PARTKEY, PS_SUPPKEY, PS_AVAILQTY,
         CASE
           WHEN PS_AVAILQTY < 100 THEN 'ORDER NOW'
           WHEN PS_AVAILQTY BETWEEN 100 AND 1000 THEN'ORDER SOON'
           ELSE 'PLENTY IN STOCK'
         END AS ORDER_STATUS
       FROM PARTSUPP
       WHERE PS_PARTKEY BETWEEN 148300 AND 148450 AND ORDER_STATUS IN ('ORDER NOW', 'ORDER SOON');
```
